What is Earth.....?

Earth is our home planet. Scientists believe Earth and its moon formed around the same time as the rest of the solar system.
They think that was about 4.5 billion years ago. Earth is the fifth-largest plan in the solar system. Its diameter is about 8,000 miles.
And Earth is the third-closest planet to the sun. Its average distance from the sun is about 93 million miles. Only Mercury and Venus are closer.
In the video on the right of your screen shows how Earth rotate around itself!

About the Earth

  • Mass : 5.97219 × 1024 kg.
  • Equatorial diameter : 12,742 km
  • surface Gravity : 9.807 m/s²
  • Average Temperature : 15°C

Some Facts about Earth

  1. Earth is the only place in the known universe confirmed to host life.
  2. It's the only one known for sure to have liquid water on its surface.
  3. It is not flat, but it's not perfectly round either..
  4. Earth takes 23.9 hr to complete 1 self rotation(1 day).
  5. It is not a perfect sphere.
